About
N
Non-fungible tokens, or NFTs, are pieces of digital information (tokens) that have a unique property seal, which, when it comes to art, is an additional protection for whoever acquires the work.
S
Tokens can be any digitally archived element, from a song to a video or photograph. The added value of NFTs is the impossibility of counterfeiting. At Galaxart, our NFTs guarantee the authenticity of the works of art found in our portal.
F
The artworks acquired as NFTs enable a unique intersection of the digital and physical worlds. Through digital frames, screens, or monitors, you can exhibit these original works of art across multiple locations simultaneously. Furthermore, if you choose to sell the art piece, its authenticity can be instantly confirmed by the new owner.
T
The importance of NFTs is increasing due to the growth of daily activities in the digital world and the creation of the Metaverse - the virtual world where more and more of us carry out personal, work and leisure activities. NFTs are the bridge that brings together the Metaverse, the digital world and the physical world.
